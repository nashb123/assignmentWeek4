Welcome to the Mojave Wildflower Society!

Who we are

We're a small community of plant-lovers dedicated to learning about, celebrating, and conserving the many unique and beautiful native plants and wildflowers found in the Mojave Desert. Made up of volunteer scientists, teachers, students, and members of the Mojave community, we welcome everyone to join our plant posse.

For generations, our mission has been to educate and learn, conserve, and train the next generation of our community to be stewards of this rich and diverse ecosystem. The Mojave Desert is not just home to thousands of plant species but is also to hundreds of endemic animal species and indigenous communities.

What we do

We focus on educational experiences aimed at our youngest desert-dwellers, but folks of all ages are welcome to our events:

  • Plant identification lessons
  • Conservation workshops
  • Beginner hikes
  • Community cleanup events
  • Hands-on activities, like planting native seeds and creating pollinator-friendly gardens

Get involved

Ready to join us? Here's how to get involved in three easy steps!

  1. Sign up for our email newsletter so you can get the latest news and event info! When you sign up, we'll also send your our Mojave 101 Beginner's Guide PDF so you can start learning ASAP.
  2. Check out our community calendar to see what's happening soon in your area! Each calendar entry contains event info and signup links.
  3. Follow us on social media to meet other members of the plant posse and view our community-curated plant and wildflower photos!
